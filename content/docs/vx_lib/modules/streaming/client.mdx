---
title: Client
description: Checks if assets exist, such as models, and loads them into memory. Throws errors if the assets are not found.
---

## vx.requestAnimDict

```lua
vx.requestAnimDict(dict, timeout)
```

- dict: `string`
- timeout?: `number`
  - Number of ticks to wait for the asset to load
  - Default: 10000
